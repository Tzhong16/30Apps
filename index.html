Dr. Louis Cha

Famous Chinese WuXia Novelist and Essayist

Dr. Louis Cha, second from left, is one of the Hongkong four wit - other three includes James Wong, Chua Lam, Ni Kuang.

Here's a time line of novels' pubilication in Dr. Louis Cha's Life

  • 1955 - The Book and the Sword
  • 1956 - Sword Stained with Royal Blood
  • 1957 - The Legend of the Condor Heroes
  • 1959 - Fox Volant of the Snowy Mountain
  • 1959 - The Return of the Condor Heroes
  • 1960 - The Young Flying Fox
  • 1961 - The Heavenly Sword and Dragon Saber
  • 1963 - Demi-Gods and Semi-Devils
  • 1964 - A Deadly Secret
  • 1966 - Ode to Gallantry
  • 1967 - The Smiling, Pround Wanderer
  • 1969 - The Deer and the Cauldron
  • 1970 - Sword of the Yue Maiden
  • 2018 - dies at age of 94.

“It’s a huge loss for Chinese people around the world, and it’s especially sad for us at Alibaba as we have embraced his writings as part of our corporate culture,”

Jack Ma, Alibaba chairman
